Why gamers choose Externo

Low CPU usage at idle

When you're not actively browsing, Externo throttles itself down. No background JS loops, no hidden processing eating into your game's CPU budget.

Lean on RAM

Chrome and Edge can eat gigabytes of RAM with a handful of tabs. Externo keeps its footprint tight so your game gets the memory it needs.

No surprise update restarts

Externo doesn't auto-update in the background and demand a restart at the worst possible moment. You update when you want to.

Instant startup

Pop over to look something up mid-session and get back in seconds. No loading spinners, no startup bloat, no waiting.

No tracking, ever

We don't collect usage data, crash reports, or anything else. Your browsing is yours. Nothing leaves your machine without you knowing.

Windows-native feel

Built specifically for Windows. It integrates cleanly with the OS, doesn't fight system resources, and behaves the way a Windows app should.
